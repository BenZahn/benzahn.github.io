Malay Cuisine

Malaysian Malay food is recognized gloablly because of it's crazy flavours with homegrown ingridients.






Nasi Lemak


Nasi Lemak is a Malaysian delicacy that has its own unique flavours with its santan flavoured rice and sweet sambal.








Dadar Gulung


Dadar gulung is a Malay dessert that consists of a green coloured crepe filled with gula Melaka coconut.





Onde-Onde


Onde-onde is a traditional green-coloured balls of rice cake filled with liquid palm sugar and coated in grated coconut.
